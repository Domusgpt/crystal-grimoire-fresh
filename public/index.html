Quick answers

Is reduced motion supported?

Yes. Motion-sensitive seekers get calm imagery, pinned sections unpinned, and the visualizer stays idle.

Do I need to install anything?

No installs. The beta runs in-browser with Firebase auth; the landing page is static and Pages-ready.

How often do builds ship?

Weekly drops during beta. Milestones follow the epitaxial states above: Seed → Epitaxy → Growth → Radiance.
